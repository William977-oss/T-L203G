orðum.


###Figure 1 consists of a graph which showed info on students via two subsequent tests with a 25 minute spacer in between, in the first test students who studied using RP (Retrieval Practice) had better results than those using SP (Study Practice) and in the second test they managed to land higher results whilst those who used SP managed to gather a lesser result. 

###One interesting fact is that stressed participants using RP (Retrieval Practice) managed to score higher to non-stressed participants on the second test.
